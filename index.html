Meet Pel Antony

Pel Antony is the founder and operator of Antony’s Financial. With over 20 years of experience in the financial services industry,

Pel has helped countless individuals, families, and businesses achieve their financial objectives.

He holds a Bachelor of Science degree and several designations and certifications, including the Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU), the Certified Health Insurance Specialist (CHS), and the Qualified Associate Financial Planner (QAFP). Pel is passionate about providing his clients with personalized financial planning services tailored to meet their unique needs and goals.

Outside of work, Pel enjoys spending time with his family and giving back to the community through volunteering and organizing community events.
